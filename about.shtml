About Gallop Racing LLC

Gallop Racing LLC is a private company based in New York (USA). Our mission is Horse Racing for Everyone, Everywhere. The company is currently experimenting with two "traditional" fantasy-style contests for the sport of thoroughbred horse racing. These are "season" long contests that consist of drafts and weekly scoring events.

Anyone can register for a stable and play along in the contest for fun. There is a global Leaderboard where stables would be listed. There is also an option to create your private Leaderboard where you would compete amongst family and friends.

There is no cost to register a stable, and there are no prizes to be paid out by Gallop Racing. We are merely facilitating the game in the hopes that it could create a broader audience of fans for horse racing. We hope by creating a contest in which casual racing fans can enjoy the sport with their family and friends, more people can experience how truly exciting a horse race can be!
