How to Play

In mid-January you'll draft 5 horses, 2 trainers, and 2 jockeys whom you think have a good chance at winning the Kentucky Derby.

There will be 7 races from Jan 22nd thru Feb 6th where you can earn points.

Then the 2nd draft of 5 horses will take place in mid-February.

There will be 10 races following that draft up until the next draft at the end of March.

The draft at the end of March is a variable draft, meaning you can take anywhere from 0-5 horses.

There will be 7 races following that draft leading up to the Derby.

The week of the Derby, you can use any of those 0-5 horse selections that you didn't use at the end of March to complete your stable.

Come Derby Day, you'll end up with 15 horses, 2 trainers and 2 jockeys in your stable.

Stables can have the same horses, trainers and jockeys as other player's stables. The opportunity to separate comes with the 3 "Power Horses" you select each week. Those horses will earn you 2x points.

As the contest goes on, the races are worth more points. So even if you stumble out of the gate, you can make up a lot of ground down the stretch!
