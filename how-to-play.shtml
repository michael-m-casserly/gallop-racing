How to Play

All Contests

Stables can have the same horses, trainers and jockeys as other player's stables. The opportunity to separate comes with the "Power Horses" you select each week. Those horses will earn you 2x points.

All horses, trainers, jockeys and sires (RTTBC only) in your stable are eligible to score points every week. There is no "starting" or "sitting/benching" of entries in your stable.

As the contest goes on, the races are worth more points. So even if you stumble out of the gate, you can make up a lot of ground down the stretch!

Kentucky Derby

In mid-January you'll draft 5 horses, 2 trainers, and 2 jockeys whom you think have a good chance at winning the Kentucky Derby.

There will be 7 races from late January thru mid-February where you can earn points.

The 2nd draft of 5 horses will take place in mid-February.

There will be 10 races following that draft up until the next draft at the end of March.

The draft at the end of March is a variable draft, meaning you can take anywhere from 0-5 horses.

There will be 7 races following that draft leading up to the Derby.

The week of the Derby, you can use any of those 0-5 horse selections that you didn't use at the end of March to complete your stable.

Come Derby Day, you'll end up with 15 horses, 2 trainers and 2 jockeys in your stable.

Horse points are awarded for the Top 4 finishers in each race. Trainer and Jockey points are only awarded to the Winner.

Breeders' Cup (7 Dirt Divisions)

In early June you'll draft 7 horses, 2 trainers, 2 jockeys, and 2 sires whom you think have a good chance of succeeding in the 7 Breeders' Cup Dirt Divisions.

There will be 21 races from early June thru early July where you can earn points.

The 1st Supplemental Draft of 5 horses will take place in early July.

There will be 16 races following that draft.

The 2nd Supplemental Draft of 5 horses will take place in late July.

There will be 14 races following that draft.

The 3rd Supplemental Draft of 5 horses will take place in mid-August.

There will be 11 races following that draft.

The 4th Supplemental Draft of 5 horses will take place in late August.

There will be 20 races following that draft.

The 5th Supplemental Draft of 5 horses will take place in early October.

There will be 21 races following that draft up until the next draft in early November.

The week of the Breeders' Cup, you will add on 3 Horses to complete your stable.

Come Breeders' Cup weekend, you'll end up with 35 horses, 2 trainers, 2 jockeys and 2 sires in your stable.

Horse points are awarded for the Top 3 finishers in each race. Trainer, Jockey and Sire points are only awarded to the Winner.
