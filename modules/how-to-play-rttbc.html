How to Play RTTBC

June
Draft seven horses, two trainers, two jockeys, and two sires.

Jul. (2x) / Aug. / Sept. / Oct.
Add 25 horses via five monthly drafts of five.
In September, you will also add one trainer, one jockey, and one sire.

November
Add final three horses for 35 total.

Earn points throughout a 20+ week (100+ race) schedule into the Breeders' Cup.
