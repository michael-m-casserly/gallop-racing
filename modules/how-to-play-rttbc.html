How to Play RTTBC

June
Draft seven horses, two trainers, two jockeys, and two sires.

Jun / Jul / Aug (3x) / Sep / Oct
Add 28 horses via seven ~monthly drafts of four. In late August, you will also add one trainer, one jockey, and one sire.

By Breeders' Cup Weekend, you'll have 35 Horses, 3 Trainers, 3 Jockeys, and 3 Sires.

Earn points throughout a 20+ week (100+ race) schedule into the Breeders' Cup.
