How to Play RTTBC

June
Draft 7 horses, 2 trainers, 2 jockeys and 2 sires.
Jul. (2x) / Aug. / Sept. / Oct.
Add on 25 horses, via 5 monthly drafts of 5. In September you will also add on 1 Trainer, 1 Jockey, and 1 Sire.
November
Add final 3 horses, for 35 total.

Earn points throughout a 20+ week (100+ race) schedule into the Breeders' Cup.
