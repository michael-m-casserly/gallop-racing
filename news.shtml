Gallop News

Gallop News sources from Twitter and various email newsletter subscriptions, including BloodHorse Magazine, the Daily Racing Form, and the Paulick Report. The focus of the news content will be whatever is relevant to the current contest. RTTR News will focus on anything related to the Kentucky Derby, and RTTBC News will focus on anything related to the Breeders' Cup Dirt Divisions.

The "Mike Casserly, Gallop Racing" Twitter account (@gallopracingllc) will serve as the News Feed on the Gallop Racing website. While you do not need to be a Twitter member to view the News Feed, if you decide to dig deeper into any of the links, you may need to create a Twitter account at some stage.

Typical re-tweet content would include horse-specific updates regarding the next races, trainer/jockey changes, injuries, etc. Weekly Race Preview videos from the DRF, as well as video replays of the RTTBC/RTTR races (from TVG, TwinSpires) would also be regularly re-tweeted.

Any news items specific to contest administration are tagged with #rttbcXX or #rttrXXX based on the current contest. For example, Road to the Breeders' Cup '22 would be #rttbc22. Items include publishing contest-specific updates, such as Nominations, Equibase Entries, RTTR/RTTBC Entries and Results, and weekly Leaderboard updates.
